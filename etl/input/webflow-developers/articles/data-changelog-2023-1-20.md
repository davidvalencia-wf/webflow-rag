## [Update to webhook retries](https://developers.webflow.com/data/changelog/2023/1/20)

Webflow will now make a maximum of 3 attempts to send a webhook event. Previously, Webflow’s servers made 5 attempts.

See [webhook limits and retries](https://developers.webflow.com/data/docs/working-with-webhooks#limits) for additional information.
