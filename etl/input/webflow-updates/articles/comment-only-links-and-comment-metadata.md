## Comment-only links
Now you can share a link so stakeholders can view and comment on your site — no login needed — they just enter their name and email when posting a comment. This will help teams speed up reviews and keep feedback in one place.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/687e8d1b96312cc631cafec7/68efdb97180b787f0b964920_commenting-bundle_share-link.jpg)
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/687e8d1b96312cc631cafec7/68efdc5cb9b4f8f83ef27b70_commenting-bundle_enter-email-name.jpg)
## Comment metadata
Parent comments now include important details like device and operating system, browser and browser version, and screen size to give you the context to address feedback efficiently without follow-ups.
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/687e8d1b96312cc631cafec7/68efdcd1cecfcf55cde7e06d_commenting-bundle_enter-email-name-1.jpg)
