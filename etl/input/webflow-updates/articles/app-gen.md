## Start building today!
App Gen is now in public beta and available on all site plans. While in beta, this functionality is free for everyone touse. Pricing and availability may change when we transition out of beta.
**_Note:_** _Apps deployed to production follow_ [_Webflow Cloud pricing_](https://webflow.com/pricing#cloud-pricing) _._
To access App Gen, enable the Webflow AI toggle in your Workspace settings. Enterprise customers will also need to enable the Webflow AI toggle for individual sites within Site settings.
Once enabled, you’ll automatically unlock App Gen along with other Webflow AI features.
