## 1. Brand Voice and Tone

### Overview
Webflow’s voice is grounded in expertise, empowerment, and authenticity. It aims to inspire professionals while remaining clear and approachable.

### Dimensions of Voice
#### 1. Knowledgeable
- **Do:** Speak confidently with authority and accuracy.
- **Don’t:** Be condescending or overly academic.
- **Principle:** *Unmistakably Pro*

#### 2. Empowering
- **Do:** Center and uplift the customer. Be optimistic and constructive.
- **Don’t:** Ignore complexity or speak negatively about challenges.
- **Principle:** *Empowering*

#### 3. Down-to-Earth
- **Do:** Use straightforward language; include subtle humor when appropriate.
- **Don’t:** Overpromise or use lofty metaphors.
- **Principle:** *Clarity & Authenticity*

#### 4. Bold
- **Do:** Be fearless and intentional with strong statements when justified.
- **Don’t:** Be brash or exaggerated.
- **Principle:** *Boldness backed by proof*

### Tone Adjustment
Voice remains constant, tone changes based on context and audience.

### Boilerplate Copy
- **One sentence:** “Webflow is a Website Experience Platform (WXP) that empowers cross-functional teams to visually build, manage, and optimize stunning websites with AI-driven personalization.”
- **Multi-sentence version:** Expands on team collaboration, enterprise scalability, and measurable outcomes.

## 2. Brand Design Guidelines

### Logo
- The **W mark** represents HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Maintain **clearspace** equal to the W’s height on all sides.
- Avoid distortion, gradients, stacking, or misuse in text.

### Typography
**Primary Font:** WF Visual Sans (Variable Font)  
- **Inspiration:** Avant Garde, Futura  
- **Weights:** Semibold (Headings), Regular (Body)  
- **Google Font Alternative:** *Poppins* (for headings), *Inter* (for body)

**Usage Rule:** Each asset should use **black, white, and one color only.** Avoid gradients or multicolor lines.

### Illustration
- Use simple, dimensional shapes with limited color.
- Keep figures technical, not cartoonish.
- Monochromatic palette (one color + black + white).

### Motion Principles
- Build: Layer and combine elements to suggest construction.
- Enhance: Zoom/crop for focus; guide the story visually.
- Reduce Friction: Transitions should be smooth, legible, and intentional.

## 3. Application for Coding Agents
When generating UI or marketing content:
- Match **typography and color tokens** from this document.
- Follow **voice and tone** when producing text or microcopy.
- Adhere to **logo spacing** and **color use restrictions**.
- Maintain **consistency** with design and motion principles.
